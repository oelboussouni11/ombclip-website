Features

Simple by design, powerful by default

Lives in your menu bar

Always accessible with a single click. No dock icon, no clutter — just a clean icon next to your wifi.

Timestamped history

Every copy is logged with the exact time, so you can always find what you copied and when.

One click to re-copy

Click any item in your history and it's instantly back on your clipboard, ready to paste.

Private & local

Everything stays on your Mac. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking. Your data is yours alone.

Lightweight

Uses barely any memory or CPU. Runs silently in the background without slowing down your Mac.

Open source

Fully transparent. Inspect the code, contribute, or fork it to make it your own.

How it works

Up and running in seconds

01

Download & install

Download the .dmg, drag the app to your Applications folder, and launch it.

02

Copy as usual

Use Cmd+C like you normally would. OMBClip automatically saves everything in the background.

03

Click to re-copy

Click the icon in your menu bar, find what you need, click it — and paste it anywhere.
